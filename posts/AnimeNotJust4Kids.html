What is Anime?

Anime is most commonly used to refer to the animations produced by the Japanese animation industry.

Why anime should not be considered as Only for kids?

Anime, is usually put under the category of kids, because it's considered to be the same as cartoons like The Mask or The Scooby-Doo show. Though, I personally believe even adults should enjoy cartoons, because:

  • They are funny and help relieve stress
  • They have really good stories
  • They may help keep the kid inside you alive

Without digressing from the topic, the reason why anime should not be considered only for kids is because:

  • There are animes which are created for adults
  • Blanket sweeping animes as for kids will result in the adults missing out on some really amazing stories and artwork.
  • It may help you broaden your imaginations.
  • I can keep going but, I believe it's better if you give it a try, starting with a few of my favourites:
    • Inuyasha series(Part 1 and 2)
    • Black Butler(Part 1)
    • Irregular at Magic Highschool
    • Naruto, Ippo, One Piece, One Punch Man, ..... to Infinity and Beyond
